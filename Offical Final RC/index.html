Ales

**Sweet and full-bodied and vary in color depending on grains used. Many contain hops which causes that bitter taste counteracting the sweet**

Pale Ale Malty and hoppy with fruity aromas
IPA Extremely bitter, amber colo
Irish Ale Malty and sweet flavors, deep red color
Amber Sweet from excess malt, amber color
Porter Darkand heavy, burnt flavor
Stout Roasted not malted, sweet with caramel aromas

Lagers

**Due to fermentation process, lagers have a crisper taste. The process is longer and at cooler temperatures which causes the beer to be mellow and smooth**

Pilsner Straw color, highly carbonated, low alcohol content
Bock Sweer and dark, wide arrange of flavors
Dunkel Dark, amber, with smooth flavors similar to coffee
